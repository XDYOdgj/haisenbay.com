Enhance Security with Durable Deadbolt Strike Plates

These deadbolt restorer strike plates are designed to tackle common door closure issues and ensure smooth operation of your deadbolt. The expanded hole feature allows the latch to throw and draw back effortlessly, preventing the need for forceful closing.

Forged from high-quality stainless steel, the plates offer a sleek silver finish that not only looks great but also ensures long-lasting durability. Ideal for use with both traditional metal deadbolts and modern electronic smart locks, these plates provide a reliable solution to door misalignment caused by wear or shrinking.

With a convenient size that accommodates seasonal ground movement, they maintain a snug fit and keep your door closing fully every time. Each pack includes a 2-piece set, making them perfect for replacing damaged plates or upgrading your existing security setup.

Installation is simple and hassle-free, with both long and short screws provided. Optional wood dowels are included to help fill oversized screw holes, ensuring a clean and secure installation process.

  • Expanded hole to prevent forceful closing
  • Sleek silver finish for long-lasting durability
  • Stronger metal construction for enhanced security
  • Helps protect door from repeated strikes
  • Includes wood dowels for easy installation

This product offers a practical, reliable, and stylish solution to common door alignment and closure problems. Whether you're dealing with an old, worn deadbolt or seeking to upgrade your door's security, these strike plates are the perfect choice.

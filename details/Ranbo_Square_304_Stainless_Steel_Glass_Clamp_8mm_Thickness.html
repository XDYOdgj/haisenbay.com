Ranbo Square Glass Clamp – Durable, Rust-Resistant, and Stylish

Elevate the look of your bathroom or shower with our high-quality square glass clamp. Designed for both functionality and aesthetic appeal, this clamping system is a must-have for any glass installation project.

Why Choose This Clamp?

Our clamp is crafted from 304 stainless steel, ensuring long-lasting durability and resistance to rust. Whether you're installing a shower door, glass panel, or sunroom window, this clamp provides a secure, reliable hold that keeps your bathroom safe and elegant.

Easy Installation

Installation is a breeze with our user-friendly design. Simply drill 1⁄2-inch holes in the glass and wall, then use the included rubber gaskets to create a perfect, snug fit. No need for complicated tools – our clamp is ready to use straight out of the box.

Modern Aesthetic

The polished chrome finish of the clamp adds a sleek, contemporary look to any bathroom design. Its minimalist style seamlessly integrates with modern interiors, making it an ideal choice for those who value both form and function.

Versatile for All Bathrooms

This clamp is perfect for a wide range of applications – from shower doors and vanity panels to screens and hinges. Its ability to fit glass ranging from 5⁄16″ to 1⁄2″ (8mm-12mm) makes it a versatile solution that suits various bathroom setups.

All-Inclusive Kit

Our clamp comes with everything you need: two clamps, gaskets, mounting screws, expansion screws, and a rubber sleeve. This ensures you can complete your installation confidently and efficiently, without worrying about missing any components.

Enhance your bathroom’s beauty and functionality with the Ranbo Square Glass Clamp – a reliable, stylish, and durable solution for every glass installation.

  • 304 Stainless Steel: Built to last with rust resistance and durability.
  • Modern Polished Chrome Design: Sleek and stylish for contemporary bathrooms.
  • Precise Fit: Compatible with glass thickness from 5⁄16″ to 1⁄2″ (8mm-12mm).
  • Factory-Tested and Easy to Install: Saves time and ensures a secure connection.
  • Versatile Use: Suitable for showers, glass panels, screens, and door hinges.

Durable Semi-Concealed Cabinet Hinges with a Stylish Brushed Nickel Finish

Enhance your kitchen and bathroom with these high-quality semi-concealed cabinet hinges. Their modern brushed nickel design adds a touch of elegance to any space, while the sturdy construction ensures they can handle daily use without compromising performance.

Featuring a self-closing mechanism with a door damper, these hinges are engineered to provide a silent and smooth closing experience. This makes them especially ideal for homes where quiet operation is a priority, like in kitchens and bathrooms.

The 15-pair set includes all the necessary components for easy installation, such as screws and a dumper. Whether you're upgrading a single cabinet or working on multiple units, these hinges offer a seamless and hassle-free fit for any frame size. With a 1/2 inch overlay dimension, they are perfect for tight spaces and heavy doors.

Known for their durability and long-lasting performance, these semi-concealed cabinet hinges provide reliable support for your cabinets. Their minimalist design and functional benefits make them a great choice for both modern and traditional home setups.

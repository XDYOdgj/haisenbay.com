Secure Your Home with the SCHLAGE Plymouth Keypad Entry

Make access to your home smarter, safer, and more convenient with the SCHLAGE Plymouth Keypad Entry. This advanced system offers keyless entry, auto-locking, and illuminated keypad to ensure comfort and security in any situation.

The keyless entry feature eliminates the need for keys, making it easier and more secure to enter your home. Once you're inside, the auto-lock function engages after 5 seconds, keeping your property protected without requiring any manual action. The keypad is illuminated for easy use at night, adding an extra layer of convenience and safety.

Programming is a breeze with the 6-digit code input, allowing you to easily add or remove access codes. The unit comes with a 9-volt battery, factory set 6-digit codes, and two pre-set 4-digit user codes, so you're ready to go right out of the box. Designed with a brushed chrome finish, it blends seamlessly with modern home aesthetics.

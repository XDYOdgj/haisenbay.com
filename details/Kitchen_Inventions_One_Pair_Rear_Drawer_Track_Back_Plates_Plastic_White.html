Durable Rear Drawer Track Back Plates for Enhanced Cabinet Stability

Upgrade your drawer support system with our high-quality rear drawer track back plates. Designed for under-mounted drawer glides, they provide a solid and secure base to ensure smooth operation and long-lasting performance in your kitchen and bathroom cabinets.

These back plates are constructed from thick, durable plastic that can handle daily use and heavy loads. With a maximum load rating of 75 lbs per pair, they are the perfect solution for homeowners seeking to reinforce their drawer slides with reliable and sturdy support.

Featuring an adjustable bracket design, they offer a wide range of lateral movement to adapt to different drawer configurations and installation needs. This versatility ensures a seamless fit and secure installation, making them ideal for both new and replacement projects.

Their modern white finish blends effortlessly with most kitchen designs, adding a touch of style without compromising on strength. Simple, clear dimension measurements are included, making it easy to install them with confidence and accuracy.

These rear drawer track back plates are the essential upgrade for anyone who values functionality and durability. Whether you're installing a new set of drawers or replacing old ones, they provide a reliable and easy solution that enhances the overall performance of your cabinet setup.
